Privacy Policy — Aipoka Photo Transfer

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Aipoka Photo Transfer ("the app") transfers photos from your phone directly to your own computer over your local WiFi network.

Data we collect

We collect no data. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no user accounts, and no cloud backend operated by the developer.

Where your photos go

Photos are sent only to the receiving computer you paired with, on your own local network. They are never uploaded to any internet server and never pass through servers operated by the developer or any third party.

Data stored on your device

  • A pairing token and the address of your receiving computer, stored locally in encrypted app storage, used only to authenticate uploads to your own PC.
  • A local list of recently synced files (shown in the app's History tab).

Uninstalling the app, or tapping Unpair, deletes this data.

Permissions

  • Photos/Media access — required to read the photos you choose to sync.
  • Camera — used only to scan the pairing QR code shown by your PC.
  • Local network access — required to reach your own computer over WiFi.

Third-party sharing

None. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
